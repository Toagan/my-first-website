Notes on Sam Altman's Key Advice on How to Be Successful

1. Compound yourself

Seek exponential growth in your career and projects. Look for opportunities with network effects and extreme scalability. Aim for each unit of work to generate more results over time.

2. Have almost too much self-belief

Cultivate strong self-belief, almost to the point of delusion. Balance this with self-awareness and truth-seeking to avoid entitlement.

3. Learn to think independently

Develop original thinking and test ideas quickly. Embrace failure as a learning opportunity and build resilience.

4. Get good at "sales"

Develop strong communication skills, especially in writing. Genuinely believe in what you're selling and practice deliberately to improve.

5. Make it easy to take risks

Take calculated risks, especially early in your career. Look for asymmetric bets with small downside but large potential upside.

6. Focus

Prioritize working on the right things over working many hours. Be relentless in accomplishing your key priorities quickly.

7. Work hard

Combine smart work with hard work to reach the top percentiles in your field. Find work you enjoy with people you like to avoid burnout.

8. Be bold

Pursue ambitious, exciting projects that matter. Follow your curiosity and don't be afraid to work on what you really want.

9. Be willful

Persist in the face of challenges and rejection. Maintain optimism and push hard to reach your goals.

10. Be hard to compete with

Develop unique skills or combinations of skills. Build personal leverage through relationships, brand, or expertise in multiple fields.

11. Build a network

Cultivate relationships with talented people. Help others generously and build a reputation for taking care of people you work with.

12. You get rich by owning things

Focus on owning equity in businesses or assets that increase rapidly in value, rather than relying solely on a salary.

13. Be internally driven

Act on your own convictions rather than to impress others. Focus on work that's intrinsically motivating and important to you.
